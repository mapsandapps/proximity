Artist's Statement

Proximity is about "the undiscovered country of the nearby." [1] Proximity encourages people to get out and explore parks, gardens, and cemeteries near them, as well as encouraging them to find nature and beauty in less-obvious locations.

[1] Robert MacFarlane

About the Artist

A native of Alabama, Mollie Taylor grew up with a deep appreciation for the rural charm of her home state. Moving to Atlanta for college at Georgia Tech presented itself with the dual challenge of a meager public transportation system and a carless existence. Coming from the woods, Mollie found a new wilderness to explore on foot. After carefully perusing some maps and a lot of trial and error, she realized that nature is much closer than most Atlantans realize.

Mollie is a grad student at the Georgia Institute of Technology where she studies how inventors collaborate across geographical space. For more information, visit mollietaylor.com.
